My name is Yanli, I was always into the arts when I was younger but lost interest as I grew older and I wasn't sure if it was something I wanted to persue until my first year of Bellevue, I researched different programs Bellevue had to offer and one that peaked my interest was the 3D program, I've never heard of 3D art until recently and as I continue to create and take the classes for this program a passion started to grow and how this kind of work tied into both my hobbies which is both arts and games. I now am currently working towards my Digital Media Arts degree and my 3D Design Certificate, while also teaching myself how to use different 3D programs and different careers that this field has to offer for me. My goals are to one day to create a website portfolio using my knowledge from this class that represents who I am, display my creations that showcase my skills, and create connections with others.
